Logic Puzzles

You might have played or heard of the classic game Minesweeper from older versions of Windows computers. Many people know about the game, but have no idea how to play it! The numbers give you information about the number mines on adjacent squares, and we can use this information to logically determine which squares contain mines.

The goal of classic Minesweeper is to determine where all of the mines are by clicking every square that does not contain a mine.

The goal of minesweeper Puzzles is to determine where all of the mines are in each puzzle, but you don't get as many hints.
